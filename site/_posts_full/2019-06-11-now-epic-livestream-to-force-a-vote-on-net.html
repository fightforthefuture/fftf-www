fftf-staff:

Today, June 11th, is the one year anniversary of the FCC repeal of net neutrality going into effect. Senators who support an open Internet are going to the floor to attempt to force a vote on the Save the Internet Act, the bill to overrule Ajit Pai and restore open Internet protections. Fight for the Future is hosting an epic, all-day livestream featuring celebrities, policy experts, small business owners, musicians, US veterans, and guest hosts reading from thousands of comments submitted by Internet users like you. Submit yours comments and then tune in at www.EpicLivestream.com.
